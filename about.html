POLICY

If you have not taken a course yet, please do not leave a review. If you are a professor, please do not leave a review. Please do not leave any personal information that could identify you, or personal information about a professor in your reviews. Please do not leave multiple reviews for a course. Please only leave reviews that describe a course as factually and truthfully as possible. Please do not link to solutions to assignments, or post code. Only finished product demonstrations will be accepted. Please do not leave inappropriate, objectionable or abusive reviews.
